## Numbering convention

- `HW-Exxx` — Electronic / Electrical
- `HW-Mxxx` — Mechanical / Structural
- `HW-Txxx` — Thermal
- `HW-Cxxx` — EMC
- `HW-Pxxx` — Power supply / Energy
- `HW-Vxxx` — Environmental (IP rating, vibration, shock)
